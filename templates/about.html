Welcome to the Portfolio Optimizer!


Participating in the financial markets is often associated with risk. The general public primarily employs the services of asset management institutions who charge high management fees and fail to provide a customized solution to their client’s needs. In order to encourage people to explore the markets themselves and make informed investments, I have attempted to develop a sophisticated portfolio optimization system that predicts the close prices of the next 3 days of US stocks and builds a customized portfolio using the bullish securities.

While there exist similar stock predicting systems, they tend to use the same set of features and parameters for all stocks. During my intensive research I realized that each stock has its own unique momentum and characteristic traits. The same set of features or learning conditions simply cannot accommodate the traits of all stocks even if they belong to the same sector. Moreover, they do not incorporate the sentiment value attached to each stock while making the prediction. In the modern age of technology, news travels at lightning rates and this influences the trading decisions of several novice traders who do not possessive intensive financial knowledge to study historical prices. Therefore, my primary aim was to setup such a system which can provide a customized approach or solution for each stock and incorporate both historical prices and sentiment scores in the decision-making process.

In my project, I first scrape the dataset for the selected stocks and build several technical indicators such as Exponential Moving Average (EMA), Relative Strength Index (RSI) etc. The system then performs Recursive Feature Elimination to extract only the important features that are relevant to the stock at hand. Over their lifetime, stocks undergo changes in their momentum and behaviour and the close prices may become more responsive to other features. Therefore, an important aspect of my project is that I perform RFE for the same stock repeatedly to capture the important features that are most relevant during a particular time period. These features are then further processed using Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and fed to deep learning models such as Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) model. The LSTM adjusts its learning rate in accordance with the stock data as stock prices change by varying magnitudes. This customized training phase allows the LSTM weights to better adapt to the stock data. The combination of RFE along with the learning rate reduction technique creates a customized learning environment for each individual stock.

As an additional point of reference, I calculate sentiment scores for each stock by using standard sentiment analysers on recent headlines. These sentiment scores help me predict those random price swings which simply cannot be supported or explained by technical indicators or price history. This combination of price prediction results and sentiment scores enables my system to withstand volatile market conditions to a great extent.

After studying the price prediction results and sentiment scores, I perform Markovitz Portfolio Optimization to build a portfolio with highest Sharpe Ratio. This portfolio is able to deliver high returns for a particular amount of risk when compared to other portfolios with similar risk. Throughout my project I have attempted to create a customized environment for each stock and recognised each stock’s individual characteristics and traits. While the financial markets still showcase events which cannot be predicted, I made a sincere attempt to create an affordable, sophisticated, and customizable system for stock prediction and portfolio optimization.
